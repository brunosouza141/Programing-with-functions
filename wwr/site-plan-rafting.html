Overview

Purpose

The main goal of this sport is to maneuver and navigate the raft through rapids or turbulent waters, often times taking you over rocks and drops, without capsizing the raft. Rivers are the veins of the Planet Earth. They have always been interesting to people, either to explore or to use them. Mostparts of rivers, usually the most beautiful parts, are hard to reach on land. Rafting allows you to enter these specialplaces. Imagine dens forests, nature, great views and much more

Audience

Why do rafting? Rafting is a great way to enjoy the outdoors and get some exercise at the same time. It is a great way to get away from the city and enjoy nature. It is also a great way to meet new people and make new friends. Is rafting dangerous? You do have a small chance of being injured on a rafting trip. What is the best time of year to go rafting? The best time of year to go rafting is in the spring and summer. The water is warmer and the weather is nicer.
